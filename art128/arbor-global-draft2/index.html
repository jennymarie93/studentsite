About Us

Our Mission is to provide the professional, expert services required to correctly and cost-effectively protect, establish, manage and factually defend proper arboriculture that will provide for a reasonably low risk, sustainable, and functionally desirable tree environment in both urban and natural sites.

Read More

Arbor Global provides clients with the foundational understanding, ability, and skill to identify and correctly apply arboricultural standards, practices, and guides that will best achieve tree and vegetation management goals. The critical basis of a successful project or program requires proper application and integration of the multiple elements of a proper design, planting stock selection, installation, establishment, and management. Well-informed application of industry standards and practices also minimizes the risk of litigation. However, if litigation challenges are encountered, Arbor Global possesses the proven experience, understanding, knowledge, and skill to provide a professional investigation that ensures well-informed actions based on facts.

Man Wa Lee
Consulting Arborist

Man Wa Lee (“Wa”) is a qualified, certified arborist, HK-0719A, with over 9 years of hands-on experience in the arboriculture industry. Wa conducts tree care, including climbing and pruning, as well as conducting educational training programs for professional tree workers and arborists, government agencies, environmental organizations, corporations, and property owners/managers in Hong Kong and the USA. Wa’s experience also includes tree preservation and protection consulting, field operations supervision, and tree health and risk assessment. Wa is fluent in both English and Cantonese providing high-level and competent language translation between local and foreign tree crews.

Tal Adelson
Consulting Arborist

Tal Adelson is an ISA Certified Arborist with more than 15 years of experience in tree care, climbing, pruning, felling and tree planting. Tal has conducted tree work and training in Hawaii, Guam, Hong Kong and the Western US. He has provided on-site, working supervision of tree workers and traffic controllers for cleanup along State Highways, at private residences and commercial properties. He possess advanced skill and experience with aerial lifts up to 80 feet, and application of climbing and advanced rigging techniques in trees over 150 feet tall.

He has also conducted education and training programs for ISA Certified Arborists and professional tree workers, as well as tree risk assessment and hazard mitigation recommendations and work, project site inspections, and supervision of contractors.

Jolie Goldenetz-Dollar
Consulting Arborist and Wildlife Biologist

Jolie Goldenetz-Dollar is a Certified Arborist, WE-4963A, and Certified Wildlife Biologist with experience in Hawaii, California, Arizona, Florida, New Jersey, Mississippi, and American Samoa. Brian Fukuda

Charley Wagner
Arborist

Charley Wagner has been chasing cows and climbing trees since the age of eight. Inspired and taught by Jeff Jepson (author of The Tree Climbers Companion), Charley grew into a competitive tree climber, storm chaser, instructor, and business owner. Over the last 23 years, Charley has worked with the USDA to eradicate Asian Longhorn beetles in New Jersey; completed hazardous tree mitigation projects in Hong Kong; executed wildfire mitigation, defensible spacing, and strategic fire line clearing for the US Forest Service; performed vegetative management safety audits and wrote climbing safety protocols for tower climbing for the International Transmission Company; and helped write a standard operating procedure (SOP) for the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE). He has also competed and served as a judge at the annual ISA International Tree Climbing Competition.
