About Us

Our Mission is to provide the professional, expert services required to correctly and cost-effectively protect, establish, manage and factually defend proper arboriculture that will provide for a reasonably low risk, sustainable, and functionally desirable tree environment in both urban and natural sites.

Arbor Global provides clients with the foundational understanding, ability, and skill to identify and correctly apply arboricultural standards, practices, and guides that will best achieve tree and vegetation management goals. The critical basis of a successful project or program requires proper application and integration of the multiple elements of a proper design, planting stock selection, installation, establishment, and management. Well-informed application of industry standards and practices also minimizes the risk of litigation. However, if litigation challenges are encountered, Arbor Global possesses the proven experience, understanding, knowledge, and skill to provide a professional investigation that ensures well-informed actions based on facts.
